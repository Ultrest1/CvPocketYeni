Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 27, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how CV Pocket ("we", "our", or "the app") handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

1. Data We Collect

CV Pocket does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All information you enter (name, contact details, work experience, education, skills, etc.) is stored exclusively on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework.

2. AI Features

CV Pocket uses Apple Foundation Models (on-device AI) available on iOS 26 and later. All AI processing happens entirely on your device. Your CV content is never sent to any external server, including Apple's servers, for AI purposes.

3. In-App Purchases

CV Pocket offers one-time in-app purchases for PDF export credits. These transactions are processed entirely by Apple via the App Store. We do not collect or store your payment information.

4. Analytics & Tracking

We do not use any third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking tools. We do not track you across apps or websites.

5. Third-Party Services

We use RevenueCat solely to manage in-app purchase validation. RevenueCat may process a pseudonymous user identifier and purchase receipt data. Please refer to RevenueCat's Privacy Policy for details.

6. Children's Privacy

CV Pocket is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
