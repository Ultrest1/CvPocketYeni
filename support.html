Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a CV?

Tap the + button on the home screen to create a new CV. Fill in your personal information, work experience, education, and skills. You can preview your CV at any time by switching to the Preview tab.

How do I export my CV as PDF?

Open your CV, switch to the Preview tab, and tap the share button (↑) in the top right corner. You'll need an export credit to share the PDF. Credits can be purchased via the in-app store.

What are export credits?

Export credits are one-time purchases — not subscriptions. One credit is used each time you successfully share a PDF. Credits never expire.

How does the AI feature work?

CV Pocket uses Apple Intelligence (on-device AI) to help you write and improve your profile summary, enhance experience descriptions, suggest skills, and tailor your CV to a job posting. This feature requires iOS 26 or later with Apple Intelligence enabled, and works completely offline — your data never leaves your device.

How do I enable the AI features?

Go to Settings → Apple Intelligence & Siri and make sure Apple Intelligence is turned on. The feature is available on iPhone 15 Pro and later.

Can I change the CV template or color?

Yes! Inside the CV editor, tap the palette icon to change the accent color, or tap the template name to browse all available templates.

Is my data private?

Absolutely. All your CV data is stored only on your device. We do not collect or upload your personal information to any server.

How do I restore my purchases?

If you reinstall the app or switch devices, your purchases can be restored. On the export screen, tap "Restore Purchases" to retrieve your previously purchased credits.
